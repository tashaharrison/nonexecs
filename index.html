Quality Non-Executive Directors from a Trusted Source:
Non-Executives Unlimited.
Non-Executives Unlimited is a joint venture between Bates Wells Braithwaite, the National Council for Voluntary and Community Organisations (NCVO) and Russam GMS and it is a solution to the problem many organisations face - trying to recruit high quality non-executives in a rigorous and yet cost effective way.

Established in 2009, Non-Executives Unlimited has quickly established itself as one of the premier providers of non-executive recruitment. We have unprecedented access to a large group of high calibre people who are actively looking for such roles and offer a unique model of delivery. With a database and network that is both large and diverse Non-Executives Unlimited is making a real difference in terms of helping boards become balanced and effective.

Non-Executives Unlimited and its sister service, Trustees Unlimited, are committed to improving governance in the growing Social Enterprise Sector and through recruitment, training and mentoring have developed a unique offering that is helping many organisations strengthen their boards.
