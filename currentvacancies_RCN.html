Royal College of Nursing Foundation (Trustees & Independent Advisors)




Trustees

RCNF is looking for 2 or 3 additional Trustees to join the Board.
The board meets 6 times a year and each appointment is for a period of up to 8 years. Meetings are in London.

A description of the role can be found here.

Trustee roles are unremunerated.

How to apply

If you think you have the experience and skills to join the Board of Trustees, please email ian.joseph@russam-gms.co.uk and copy emma.kynoch@russam-gms.co.uk with the subject ‘Trustee’ in the subject line and provide the following:

  • a supporting statement, explaining how you believe your skills and experience match the requirements of the role,
  • a comprehensive CV

We would also be grateful if you could provide us with the details of two referees. Referees will not, of course, be contacted without your prior consent.

The closing date for applications is Monday 17th September. Trustees Unlimited will be forwarding all applications to the RCNF.

Independent Advisers

RCNF is also looking for two or three independent advisers to join each of its expert committees to provide them with independent and objective specialist advice. 
Specifically the charity is looking for independent advisers:

  • with experience of running a Benevolent Fund/providing a welfare advisory service;
  • who have sat on grants committees or delivered a grant giving service;
  • with a fundraising/marketing background to help the Income Generation Committee to deliver our fundraising strategy; and
  • with an HR background and experience of making non-executive appointments

General information on the role of Independent Advisors can be found here.

Terms of reference for each of the sub committees can be found by clicking through the appropriate link below:

  • Grants
  • Appointments
  • Benevolent Fund
  • Income Generation

The role of an independent adviser requires a commitment of approximately 8-10 days a year and is for a period of up to 5 years. Committee meetings are held in London four times a year.

All the roles are unremunerated.

How to Apply

If you think you have the experience and skills to join one of the Foundation’s specialist committees, please email ian.joseph@russam-gms.co.uk and copy emma.kynoch@russam-gms.co.uk and provide the following with the name of the committee you wish to be considered for in the subject line and provide the following:

  • a supporting statement, explaining how you believe your skills and experience match the requirements of the role,
  • a comprehensive CV

We would also be grateful if you could provide us with the details of two referees. Referees will not, of course, be contacted without your prior consent.

The closing date for applications is Monday 17th September. Trustees Unlimited will be forwarding all applications to the RCNF.
